Minimum fund requirement is Rs. 12,00,000 and Rs. 25,00,000 respectively for rural ROs and regular ROs. 

This fund can be in the form of 

savings accounts, 
deposits with bank/registered companies/postal schemes 
OR 
bonds 
OR 
shares of listed companies in Demat form OR 
mutual funds OR 
National Savings Certificates, etc. 

Please note that only 60% of the certified value of Shares, Mutual funds & Bonds will be taken into consideration for eligibility purpose
